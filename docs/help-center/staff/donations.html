What this page is for

Use this guide for the stable day-to-day donation workflow: search and review donation activity, record new gifts, understand payment states, and confirm that receipts were sent.

Before you start

  • Know whether you are reviewing an existing donation or entering a new one.
  • Have the donor, amount, payment method, and payment status ready.
  • Search first if you are not sure whether the donation already exists.

Steps

  1. Start on the Donations list. Review the total donation amount, average donation, and total count before you drill into a specific record.
  2. Use search and quick filters. Quick filters such as Completed, Pending, and Credit Card are helpful for first-pass review. Move to the full filters if you need finer control.
  3. Record a donation from the main action. Use Record Donation for staff-entered gifts and keep payment method and payment status accurate from the start.
  4. Open the detail page for verification. Use the donation detail page to confirm payment status, payment method, and receipt information.
  5. Use receipt history intentionally. The receipt area is where staff confirm whether a receipt has been sent and when that happened.
  6. Use the payment flow only when your organization collects card payments in the app. For normal manual entry, stay in the donation record flow instead of starting a payment session.
Screenshot: Donations list with quick filters and status chips

Tip: If the donation list looks wrong, clear your filters first. This page can remember filters in your browser.

What happens next

When donation records are accurate, totals update in dashboard views, reports reflect the right payment states, and finance follow-up becomes more reliable.

Common mistakes

  • Treating a pending payment like a failed payment.
  • Recording the same gift twice when you did not search first.
  • Forgetting that list filters may still be active from earlier work.
  • Marking a receipt process complete without checking the donation detail page.

Need help?

If a payment result is unclear, do not assume the donation is lost or complete. Capture the donation record, payment state shown on screen, and any transaction or receipt detail before you escalate to a finance or admin contact.
